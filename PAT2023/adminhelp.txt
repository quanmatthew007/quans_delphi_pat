Admin Help:

Buttons: 
Each button executes what is labeled on it,
if you hover your pointer(mouse) over the button,
it will usually provide extra information.

Links:
when clicking on a link(it will be in form of a label
stating 'click here') it will take you out of the program
and direct you into a website on a browser.(data charges may apply)


Fish Information form:
-when searching for a fish, ensure that you enter its name
in the correct spelling and case
-Nicknames and impropernames of fish is not applicable when entered
-when leaving a comment, ensure that it contains the following:
  -your username(or anon)
  -your comment
  -Your comment which is less than 8 lines
-if there is an error in the comment or there is a need to remove it,
please contact the admin from our instagram page.

Catches form:
-when entering a catch please ensure that the information entered is valid
-should you come across any invalid information please make 
use of the view and update buttons.

-do not type in your option but merely click on the arrow and select 
your option
